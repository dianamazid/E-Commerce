About The Carpool Company

Let's Play Pick-Up!

Everybody hates traffic, so what better way to get rid of traffic then carpooling with others to nearby destinations. The Carpool Company is a carpool service for UVA students to find rides to and from a selected location. Needless to say, many students at UVA come from similar places. With that being said, this allows students without cars to find rides home during the holidays or breaks, along with rides to visit other friends at other schools. This website provides a cheap, easy and quick way for students to find the rides they need.

The Carpool Company contains profiles for every user so that the drivers and riders are able to rate their ride based on conversation, safeness, punctuality, etc. The reason for this is to provide safety for all rides and drivers. In addition, drivers not only post availability for spots and the time they are leaving, but also the price they will be charging each passenger to help compensate for gas.

Our company values honesty, integrity, and an all around fun environment, which is shown through our slogan. Sign up now and become a part of reducing traffic, saving money, and making new friends!
